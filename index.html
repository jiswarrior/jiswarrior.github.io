Understanding Wristbands, Stamps & Horizon Play
// FH6 runs three parallel progression systems simultaneously
Wristbands — Horizon Festival
Yellow — qualify at the Invitational
Green — complete road & dirt events
Blue, Pink, Orange — race progression
Purple — 32,500 Festival Points needed
Gold — become Horizon Legend
⚑ Unlocks Legend Island
Stamps — Discover Japan
Explore regions, smash mascots
Street & Touge races, food deliveries
7 stamp levels (Yellow → Gold)
Each level unlocks new Barn Find rumors
Gold stamp = Master Explorer
⚑ Unlocks all 15 Barn Finds
Horizon Play — Multiplayer
Spec Racing online events
Touge Showdown (1v1 mountain duel)
Eliminator, Hide & Seek, Custom Racing
XP feeds its own Horizon Play rank
Fully separate from campaign
⚑ Independent multiplayer progression
Resets every Thursday
