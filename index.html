📌 Wordle Winning Strategy & Guide

  • Use unique letters in your first 2 guesses to eliminate fast
  • Move yellow letters to new positions on the next guess
  • Never reuse gray letters — they are not in the target word
  • Lock vowels A E I O U early in your Wordle strategy
  • Avoid rare words to conserve your 6 guesses

Following a consistent Wordle guide can help you improve your average guess score and maintain long win streaks.
